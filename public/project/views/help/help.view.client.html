What is NutritionPlanner?

NutritionPlanner is an online web application food explorer and trackers. Shoppers could search for store food products to learn ingredients and nutrients knowledge.

Athletes, dietitians or users who are in fitness or on a diet could track the details of one food. Users could set up their own profile as well.


Search Food Product or Recipe

Clicking "Search for nutrients of food" on homepage, you could search for grocery food product or recipe by entering the key words. If there are matched food, they will be showed below. Browse the list and click on " view product" to view details including nutrients fact.


Sign in, Sign up and Sign out

From the top navigation bar, click on Sign in to login to NutritionPlanner or click on Sign up to register a new NutritionPlanner account. After login, you could access to profile page. To sign out, click on Sign out next to username in the navigation bar.
